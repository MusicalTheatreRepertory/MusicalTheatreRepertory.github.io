Producer + Music Coordinator

Namratha Kasalanati

Namratha V. Kasalanati (she/her) is a composer, pianist, cellist, and community activist, currently pursuing her Bachelor of Music in Composition and a Bachelor of Science in Economics/Mathematics at USC. Hailing from the Bay Area, her work aims to bring attention to the diverse musical traditions around the world, focusing on the intersections between Western and Indian classical music. Some of her notable composition teachers have been Dr. Donald Crockett, Dr. Veronika Krausas, Brian Head, and Camae Ayewa (Moor Mother). She has also studied conducting with Sharon Lavery and orchestration with Dr. Frank Ticheli, Dr. Juan Pablo Contreras, and Mark Weiser. Beyond the classical sphere, she has composed for fifteen short films, one of which was screened at the Ojai Film Festival in 2023. This is her fourth MTR production, her second time producing, and she’s so inspired by the Anastasia team’s creativity, passion, and . In her spare time, she loves to read fantasy novels, triple jump, and enjoy a pack of Sour Skittles (or Gummy Clusters) every now and then.
